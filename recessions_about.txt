Column name: Explanation
------------------------
isodate: Date
year: Year
quarter: Quarter of the year
recession: Was the US economy in recession? 1 yes 0 no. https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/JHDUSRGDPBR
r_index: Recession Indicator Index. Probability the US is in recession in a particular quarter. Runs from 0 to 100.https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/JHGDPBRINDX
l_index: Leading Index. Quarterly average of the monthly leading index. Negative values indicate recessions. Starts in 1982. https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/USSLIND
dowjones: Close of the Dow Jones Industrial Average. https://stooq.com/q/d/?s=%5Edji&c=0&d1=19670101&d2=20200903&i=q
election: Was there a US presidential election that year? 1 yes 0 no
btm50: National Income Share of the bottom 50% (annual data). Percent. https://wid.world/data/#countrytimeseries/sptinc_p0p50_992_j;sptinc_p99p100_992_j/US/1913/2019/eu/k/p/yearly/s
top1: National Income Share of the top 1% (annual data). Percent. https://wid.world/data/#countrytimeseries/sptinc_p0p50_992_j;sptinc_p99p100_992_j/US/1913/2019/eu/k/p/yearly/s
